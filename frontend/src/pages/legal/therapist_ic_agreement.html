1 Nature of Relationship
🤝

Independent Contractor

The Provider is engaged as an independent contractor. No employer-employee, partnership, joint venture, agency, franchise, or fiduciary relationship is created.

🧠

Clinical Autonomy

The Provider retains full and exclusive clinical autonomy. MANAS360 does not direct, control, or interfere with clinical decision-making, diagnosis, treatment, or prescriptions.

💰

Tax Responsibility

The Provider is solely responsible for income tax, GST (if applicable), professional tax, and all statutory contributions required under Indian law.

📋

No Entitlements

The Provider is not entitled to employee benefits, PF, ESI, gratuity, or any other statutory benefits applicable to employees.

2 Provider Representations & Eligibility
2.1 The Provider represents and warrants that they hold all of the following at all times during the engagement:
(a) A valid degree in psychology, psychiatry, counselling, psychotherapy, or a related field from a recognised institution;
(b) Valid and current registration with the Rehabilitation Council of India (RCI), National Medical Commission (NMC), relevant State Medical Council, or other applicable regulatory authority;
(c) Professional indemnity insurance (recommended) or willingness to participate in MANAS360's group insurance programme;
(d) No pending disciplinary actions, licence suspensions, or criminal charges related to professional conduct.
3 Services
3.1 The Provider agrees to offer professional mental health services via the MANAS360 Platform, including video therapy, audio therapy, chat-based therapy, group sessions, and clinical assessments.
3.2 The Provider shall maintain an up-to-date availability calendar on the Platform and honour all confirmed bookings.
3.3 The Provider shall maintain complete and accurate session notes using MANAS360's SOAP/Progress Note templates within 24 hours of each session.

6 Provider Obligations
6.1 Maintain valid and subsisting professional qualifications, licences, and registrations at all times, and immediately notify MANAS360 of any suspension, restriction, lapse, investigation, or change in registration status.
6.2 Comply with all applicable laws, including the Mental Healthcare Act, 2017, Telemedicine Practice Guidelines, 2020, NMC Code of Ethics, and DPDPA 2023.
6.3 Attend mandatory platform training and quarterly compliance updates.
6.4 Respond to patient booking requests within four (4) hours during declared availability and maintain professional responsiveness and continuity of care.
6.5 Not solicit, divert, or attempt to move MANAS360 patients for off-platform services or share personal contact information with patients.
6.6 Report all incidents — including clinical emergencies, patient complaints, data breaches, and ethical concerns — to MANAS360 within 24 hours.
6.7 Maintain strict confidentiality of all patient information and access, use, store, or disclose such information solely for lawful clinical purposes.
6.8 Implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect personal data against unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, loss, or misuse, in accordance with DPDPA 2023 and the Platform's privacy and security policies.
7 Platform Obligations
7.1 Provide a reliable and secure technology infrastructure to support teleconsultation services, with a targeted service availability of 99.5% uptime, subject to scheduled maintenance, force majeure events, and factors beyond its reasonable control.
7.2 Manage patient acquisition initiatives, marketing activities, payment processing mechanisms, and billing administration in connection with services delivered through the Platform.
7.3 Provide integrated session management tools, standardised SOAP note templates, prescription management functionality (limited to licensed psychiatrists where applicable), and access to clinical dashboards.
7.4 Process payouts as per Section 5 of this Agreement.
7.5 Provide professional indemnity insurance coverage under a group policy arrangement on an optional basis, with applicable premium costs to be shared as mutually agreed.
8 Liability & Indemnification
8.1 The Provider shall be solely liable for clinical negligence, malpractice, or breach of professional standards arising out of services rendered through the Platform.
🚫 The Provider agrees to indemnify and hold harmless MANAS360 from any claims, losses, or liabilities arising out of:
   • Clinical misconduct or malpractice;
   • Regulatory violations or licence breaches;
   • Misrepresentation of credentials;
   • Breach of patient confidentiality or data protection obligations.

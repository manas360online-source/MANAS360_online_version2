1 Confidential Information
1.1 "Confidential Information" includes all of the following categories:
🏥
Category (a)

Patient Data

All patient data, health records, assessment scores, session notes, and personal information.

💼
Category (b)

Business Information

MANAS360 business plans, commercial strategies, technology, algorithms, pricing, and strategies.

👥
Category (c)

Provider Network Data

Provider network data including other Providers' identities and performance metrics.

🔒
Category (d)

Any Other Sensitive Info

Any information marked as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential.

2 Obligations
✅ Provider SHALL
(a) Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential at all times.
(b) Use Confidential Information solely for providing professional services through the MANAS360 Platform.
(c) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent.
(d) Implement reasonable and industry-standard security measures to protect Confidential Information.
🚫 Provider SHALL NOT
(a) Download, export, copy, store, or transmit patient data to personal devices or external systems.
(b) Discuss patient cases with persons not involved in the patient's care.
(c) Use patient data for research, publication, or teaching without explicit written consent and institutional ethics approval.
(d) Retain patient data after termination of this Agreement.
3 Exceptions
3.1 Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that meets any of the following criteria:
(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Provider;
(b) Was known to the Provider prior to disclosure by MANAS360;
(c) Is independently developed by the Provider without reference to any Confidential Information;
(d) Is required to be disclosed by law, court order, or regulatory authority — provided the Provider gives MANAS360 prompt written notice before disclosure.
4 Patient Data Handling
🖥️ 4.1 All patient data must be accessed exclusively through the MANAS360 Platform. Downloading, exporting, or screen-capturing patient data is strictly prohibited unless explicitly authorised.
📝 4.2 Session notes must be entered directly into the MANAS360 Platform and must not be stored locally on any personal or external device.
🔐 4.3 All video/audio sessions are encrypted end-to-end. The Provider shall not use any recording software during sessions under any circumstances.

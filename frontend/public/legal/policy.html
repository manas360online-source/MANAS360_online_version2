3 Purpose of Data Processing
3.1We process your data for the following purposes with your consent:
(a)Enabling you to access or use the Platform;
(b)Providing Platform services, including matching you with Providers;
(c)Processing payments and subscriptions;
(d)Delivering AI-powered mental health support;
(e)Tracking clinical outcomes (PHQ-9/GAD-7 trends) for your benefit and your Provider's reference;
(f)Sending notifications (session reminders, booking confirmations, wellness tips);
(g)Improving Platform quality, features, and user experience;
(h)Complying with legal obligations under Indian law;
(i)Crisis detection and referral to emergency services when imminent risk is identified.
4 Legal Basis for Processing
4.1Consent (DPDPA Section 6): We process most data based on your free, specific, informed, unconditional and unambiguous consent obtained during registration and at relevant points of data collection.
4.2Legitimate Uses (DPDPA Section 7): Certain processing is undertaken for legitimate uses including compliance with legal obligations, responding to medical emergencies involving threat to life or health, preventing fraud or misuse of the Platform, and employment-related processing for Providers.
ℹ️ Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing conducted prior to withdrawal. To withdraw, contact dpo@manas360.com.
5 Data Storage & Security
5.1Location: All personal data is stored on AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) servers located in India, ensuring compliance with DPDPA data localisation requirements.
5.2Encryption: All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
5.3Access Controls: Role-based access controls (RBAC) restrict data access to authorised personnel only. Providers can only access data of patients assigned to them.
5.4Audit Trails: All material access to personal data is logged, including timestamp, user identity, and action performed.

6 Data Sharing
6.1We share data ONLY in the following circumstances:
(a)With your Provider: Relevant session history, assessment scores, and clinical data are shared with the Provider you are matched with, solely for the purpose of providing therapy;
(b)Payment Processors: Transaction data is shared with PhonePe for payment processing;
(c)Legal Obligations: When required by law, court order, or government authority;
(d)Emergency: If we reasonably believe there is imminent risk of serious harm to you or others, we may disclose relevant information with emergency services or crisis helplines;
(e)Anonymised Research: De-identified, aggregated data may be used for mental health research subject to ethical safeguards and approvals.
✅ We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.
🚫 We do NOT share your personal data with advertisers.

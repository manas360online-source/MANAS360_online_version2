Acceptable Use Policy
Document: Acceptable Use Policy (A5) Applies to: All Platform Users Governed by: Laws of India
1 Purpose
1.1  This Policy sets forth the acceptable and prohibited uses of the MANAS360 Platform and is intended to ensure a safe, respectful, supportive, and therapeutically appropriate environment for all Users.
2 Prohibited Activities
2.1 Users shall NOT engage in any of the following activities:
🚫
Clause (a)

Use the Platform for any purpose other than seeking, providing, or supporting legitimate mental health or wellness services.

⚖️
Clause (b)

Use the Platform for unlawful, fraudulent, deceptive, or harmful activities.

😠
Clause (c)

Harass, threaten, abuse, stalk, intimidate, or exploit any User, Provider, or Platform personnel.

🔞
Clause (d)

Share sexually explicit, pornographic, violent, self-harm-promoting, or otherwise harmful content unrelated to therapeutic objectives.

🎭
Clause (d)

Impersonate a mental health professional, medical professional, or any other person.

💸
Clause (e)

Attempt to contact Providers outside the Platform for the purpose of circumventing Platform fees.

📤
Clause (f)

Share, distribute, or sell any therapeutic content, session recordings, or proprietary content from the Platform.

📢
Clause (g)

Use the Platform to promote products, services, or ideologies unrelated to mental health.

🦠
Clause (h)

Introduce viruses, malware, ransomware, spyware, or any harmful code into the Platform.

🕷️
Clause (i)

Collect, scrape, crawl, extract, or harvest data from the Platform without authorisation.

🤖
Clause (j)

Create fake accounts, bots, scripts, or automated systems to interact with the Platform.

🧠
Clause (k)

Use AI-generated content to falsely represent human interaction in group sessions.

3 Enforcement
3.1 Violations of this Policy may result in one or more of the following actions, depending on severity and recurrence:
ActionDescriptionSeverity
Written Warning Formal notification of the violation with a request to cease the prohibited conduct Low
Temporary Suspension Access to the Platform suspended for a defined period pending review Medium
Permanent Ban Irreversible termination of account access across all Platform services High
Legal Proceedings Civil or criminal action initiated against the User in accordance with applicable law Critical
Law Enforcement Referral Disclosure of relevant information to appropriate law enforcement authorities Critical
3.2 MANAS360 reserves the right to investigate suspected violations and take appropriate action without prior notice.
3.3 Enforcement decisions shall be made at MANAS360's reasonable discretion, based on the severity, recurrence, and risk to Platform safety posed by the violation.
⚠️ MANAS360 reserves the right to preserve and share any evidence of prohibited activity with law enforcement or legal authorities where required by applicable law or to protect the safety of Users, Providers, or the Platform.
4 Reporting Violations
4.1 Users are encouraged to report any suspected policy violations through the following channels:
🚩

How to Report

In-App Use the Report feature available on any session, profile, or content screen
Email report@manas360.com
Support Contact our support team via the Help Centre within the Platform
ℹ️ All reports are treated with strict confidentiality. MANAS360 does not disclose the identity of the reporting User to the subject of the report unless required by law.
5 Changes to This Policy
5.1 We may update this Policy periodically to reflect legal, technical, or operational changes. Material changes will be notified via email and in-app notification. Continued accessing or using the Platform after notification constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

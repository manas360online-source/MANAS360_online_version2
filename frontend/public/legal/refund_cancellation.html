Refund & Cancellation Policy
Document: Refund & Cancellation Policy (A7) Applies to: All Paid Services Governed by: Laws of India
1 Purpose & Scope
1.1  This Refund & Cancellation Policy governs all payments made by Users while accessing or using the MANAS360 Platform, including therapy sessions, subscriptions, assessments, digital programs, and other paid services.

1.2  By booking a session, purchasing a subscription, or making any payment on the Platform, the User agrees to be bound by this Policy.
2 Session Cancellations
✅
Clause 2.1 · By Patient
More than 24 hours before session

Full refund to original payment method within 5–7 business days.

💚 Full Refund ⏱ 5–7 Business Days
⚡
Clause 2.2 · By Patient
12 to 24 hours before session

50% refund to original payment method. Remaining 50% credited as MANAS360 wallet balance.

💛 50% Refund 💙 50% Wallet Credit
⏰
Clause 2.3 · By Patient
Less than 12 hours before session

No refund. Full amount credited as MANAS360 wallet balance, valid for 90 days.

❌ No Refund 💙 100% Wallet Credit (90 days)
🚫
Clause 2.4 · By Patient
No-show (no cancellation made)

No refund. Provider receives full session fee as compensation for reserved time.

❌ No Refund
👤
Clause 2.5 · By Provider
Provider cancels session

Full refund to patient's original payment method. Provider's reliability score may be affected.

💚 Full Refund to Patient
⚙️
Clause 2.6 · Technical Failure
Platform-side technical failure

Full refund or complimentary rescheduled session — at patient's choice.

💚 Full Refund 🔄 Free Reschedule
3 Subscription Cancellations
ScenarioOutcome
Cancel at any time Subscription remains active until end of current billing period. No prorated refund for unused days.
Annual plan — cancel within first 14 days Full refund minus the cost of any sessions attended during that period.

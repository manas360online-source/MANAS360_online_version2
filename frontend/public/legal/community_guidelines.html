1 Purpose & Applicability
These Community Guidelines govern all Users and Providers who access or participate in group therapy sessions, peer-support forums, moderated discussions, or any other interactive community feature made available on the MANAS360 Platform. These Guidelines operate in addition to, and shall be read together with, the Terms of Service, Acceptable Use Policy, and Privacy Policy; in the event of any inconsistency, the stricter provision shall prevail.

Participation in community features is a privilege and not an inherent right, and MANAS360 reserves the sole discretion and authority to restrict, suspend, or revoke access where necessary to preserve therapeutic integrity, community safety, and compliance with applicable standards.

3 Guidelines
🤝
Guideline 3.1
Be Respectful

Treat every member with dignity and respect. Diverse backgrounds, experiences, identities, and perspectives are welcomed and valued.

🔒
Guideline 3.2
Maintain Confidentiality

What is shared in group sessions stays in group sessions. Do not share other members' personal stories, identities, or health information outside the group. Unauthorised disclosure may constitute a violation of applicable data protection laws.

💬
Guideline 3.3
Use Supportive Language

Avoid judgment, criticism, or unsolicited advice. Use "I" statements when sharing personal experiences to keep communication grounded and non-blaming.

🚧
Guideline 3.4
Respect Boundaries

If someone does not wish to share, respect their choice. Do not pressure, interrogate, or coerce others into disclosure of personal information.

🚫
Guideline 3.5
No Promotion or Solicitation

Do not promote or solicit products, services, alternative treatments, financial schemes, religious campaigns, or political ideologies within any community space.

🚨
Guideline 3.6
Crisis Protocol

If a member expresses suicidal thoughts or imminent danger, alert the session facilitator immediately. Do not attempt to manage a crisis independently. Facilitators may activate internal escalation protocols where required.

🎙️
Guideline 3.7
One Voice at a Time

In group sessions, allow each person to speak without interruption. Active, patient listening is as important as sharing.

⏺️
Guideline 3.8
No Recording

Audio recording, video recording, livestreaming, screenshot capture, transcription, or external broadcasting of sessions or forums is strictly prohibited.
